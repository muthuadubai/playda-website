# Vetrix Career Institute — Launch & Operations Plan (Sankarankovil)

## 1) Institute Overview
- **Name:** Vetrix Career Institute
- **Location:** Sankarankovil (facility currently being finalized)
- **Launch date:** 15 Jun 2026
- **Mode:** Offline centre (students attend) + Online remote faculty (live teaching on TV in the centre)
- **Target batch size:** 30 students
- **Student segment:** 11th + 12th (combined batch)
- **Fee:** ₹5,000 per month per student
- **Language:** English teaching + spoken Tamil support onsite
- **Ownership:** Proprietorship
- **Bank options:** City Union Bank (CUB) or Axis
- **Rent assumption:** ₹10,000/month; advance ₹1,00,000
- **Setup budget:** ₹5,00,000 (extendable up to ₹8,00,000)

---

## 2) Core Differentiator (Positioning)
Vetrix is positioned as:
- **Remote expert faculty + local daily supervision + disciplined study environment + mandatory weekly tests + parent reporting.**
- Students get a **“study-library + coaching”** model, not just lectures.

---

## 3) Centre Timings (Final)
- **Weekdays (Mon–Fri):** 4:00 PM – 10:00 PM
- **Weekends (Sat–Sun):** 10:00 AM – 9:00 PM
- **Mandatory academic window (all students):** 6:00 PM – 9:00 PM
- Other hours are **optional but supervised study** (high value add).

---

## 4) Teaching Model (Alternate-Day Online + Study-Hall)
### Objective
- Give students time to **complete DPP + revise** between teaching days.
- Reduce remote faculty live hours and keep costs manageable at ₹5k/month.

### Weekly structure
- **Mon / Wed / Fri:** Online teaching (live remote faculty)
- **Tue / Thu:** Supervised study-hall + doubt clearing + submissions
- **Sat:** Revision + pre-test drill + backlog clearance
- **Sun:** Mandatory weekly test + analysis + improvement blocks

## 6) Open-Hours Study Library Plan (4–6 PM + 9–10 PM + Weekends)
### Purpose
- Provide structured self-study time so results improve.
- Increase parent trust (“my child studies in a safe place daily”).

### Study lanes (in the classroom)
- **Lane A: DPP Completion Lane**
- **Lane B: NCERT Reading Lane (especially Biology)**
- **Lane C: Error-Log Lane (wrong questions + concept fix)**

### Entry/Exit rule (simple enforcement)
- Every student entering open hours writes:
  - Goal for the session (example: “Complete Bio DPP-14 + revise NCERT Ch. 7 lines”)
- Exiting requires showing:
  - Work completed (not perfect, but real output)

---

## 7) Academic System (Quality Control)
### Daily deliverables
- DPP assigned on teaching day
- DPP completion + checking on study-hall day
- Error-log updated daily

### Weekly deliverables
- Mandatory Sunday test
- Weekly report to parents (attendance + marks + weak chapters)
- Faculty review call (30 minutes) to adjust plan

### Material strategy (cost-effective)
- NCERT-first (Bio + Chem)
- NEET MCQs for application
- Vetrix material limited to:
  - DPP sheets
  - Weekly tests + answer keys
  - Error-log format + revision tracker

---

## 8) Batch Handling (Combined 11th + 12th)
### Approach
- Teach common concepts together
- Split practice into 2 levels:
  - **11th Track:** concept-building + easier NEET MCQs
  - **12th Track:** NEET-level MCQs + higher difficulty set

### Testing
- Either:
  - Same test paper but different syllabus scope for 11th, OR
  - Two versions (preferred once you have stable operations)
